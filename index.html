About Us

G-Space Interiors — Where Your Dream Home Becomes Reality

G-Space Interiors is Madambakkam's premier luxury interior design and contracting firm. Led by Mr. Gopalakrishnan, we are a one-stop destination for complete home transformation — from concept to move-in. With a perfect 5-star reputation and a passion for craftsmanship, we deliver spaces that inspire.

Our signature promise: every project is completed within 45 days, backed by a lifetime warranty and zero hidden costs. From modular kitchens to full home interiors, we handle every detail — woodwork, electrical, plumbing — through dedicated specialist teams.
