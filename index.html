Our Story

G-Space Interiors

G-Space Interiors, led by the dedicated Mr. Gopalakrishnan, is a premier interior design and contracting firm based in Madambakkam, Chennai. With a perfect 5-star reputation built on 76 glowing reviews, we specialize in turning ordinary living spaces into beautifully crafted, functional environments tailored to each client's vision. From custom wardrobes and TV units to prayer units and crockery shelves, we bring precision, creativity, and professionalism to every project.

What sets G-Space Interiors apart is our commitment to being your single point of contact for all interior needs — whether it's woodwork, PVC installations, electrical work, or plumbing. Our dedicated teams for each vertical ensure that every aspect of your home renovation is handled with expertise and care. We work with minimal disruption to your daily life, completing projects cleanly and efficiently while keeping you informed every step of the way.

  • Perfect 5-Star Rating across 76 verified customer reviews
  • Single point of contact for all interior, electrical, and plumbing needs
  • Dedicated specialist teams for each vertical ensuring expert workmanship
